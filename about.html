Our Story

When we designed the Pine city mall and established our heart's desire to bring our visitors' convenience and experiences. Pine city mall is built using the latest technologies in the retail sector from clothing stores incorporating AI technology linking consumers to their ideal clothing sizes and fashion preferences. All our restaurants operate with self-cleaning bots. Orders are placed using electronic menus , linked to each restaurant's system. Restaurants operate in smart kitchens, ensuring that every food item on the menu can be made, our smart kitchens alerts when ingredients are running low to ensure there is always a supply for the restaurant's demand. All minors are given tracking bracelets upon entry and synced with their parent's devices. This is to ensure safety and a more relaxed mall experience. We believe that technology is the future , however, the future needs to be conserved with eco-friendly actions taken today. We recycle and encourage this to all our partners. Please feel free to visit our information center and make use of our personal guided tours to get the full Pine City Mall experience.
